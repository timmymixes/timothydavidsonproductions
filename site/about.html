About

Hey, I'm Timothy.

Thank you for checking out my site! I am a cinematographer based in OKC. I have been shooting most of my whole life but started shooting semi-professionally in 2016. Wedding films are my specialty, but I have experience in a variety of shooting.

I graduated from Southern Nazarene University in 2014 with a degree in Music Business. Audio engineering has been my main career since 2012, and I have had the privilege of mixing several local artists — my catalog of mixing can be found here.

I would love to get to work with you on your wedding or project. Please either call me or fill out my contact form and I will get in touch with you soon.
